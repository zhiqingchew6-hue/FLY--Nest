Narita Gateway Hotel

4.0 ★★★★
  • Introduction
  • History

Property Description

Stay just minutes from the airport at Narita Gateway Hotel — your smooth and stylish gateway to Japan. Located roughly a 10-minute drive from Narita International Airport and with free shuttle service to both the airport and Narita Station, this hotel puts convenience first.

Step inside to discover 307 elegantly furnished rooms, each equipped with air conditioning, free high‑speed Wi‑Fi, a flat‑screen TV, a mini-fridge or electric kettle, and a private bathroom — so you can relax and recharge after your journey.

Wake up to a hearty buffet breakfast at the on‑site restaurant, then choose between staying active in the 24‑hour gym or strolling in the hotel's peaceful Japanese garden. For guests coming by car, there's parking available (with space for up to 100 cars); for those traveling light, the shuttle bus offers an easy ride to the airport terminals.

Whether you're arriving on an early flight, making a quick stopover, or beginning a longer journey in Japan — Narita Gateway Hotel combines comfort, convenience, and Japanese hospitality to make your stay effortless and enjoyable.

History

Narita Gateway Hotel, opened in 1992 in Narita, Japan, has served travelers and business guests for decades. It changed names several times, becoming Narita Gateway Hotel in 2013, and briefly served as a facility for mild COVID‑19 cases before resuming normal hotel operations.
