Seoul

N Seoul Tower

4.0 ★★★★
Introduction ▶
Introduction Top Attractions History Hotels Traditional Food

N Seoul Tower is a famous observation and communication tower located on Namsan Mountain in central Seoul. It is one of the city’s most iconic landmarks and offers panoramic views of Seoul and its surrounding areas. The tower attracts tourists and couples, especially for sightseeing, photography, and romantic experiences.

Top Attraction

N Seoul Tower features observation decks where visitors can enjoy 360-degree views of Seoul, including the Han River, city skyline, and nearby mountains. The tower is also famous for “Locks of Love”, where couples attach padlocks to symbolize their love. At the base, there are restaurants, cafés, and gift shops, providing a full visitor experience. The tower is beautifully illuminated at night, making it a popular spot for evening visits and city nightscape photography.

History

N Seoul Tower was completed in 1971 as a communication and broadcast tower. Over time, it was transformed into a tourist attraction with observation decks, restaurants, and cultural installations. Today, N Seoul Tower stands as a symbol of Seoul, combining modern architecture, romantic attractions, and breathtaking views, and remains one of the city’s top destinations for both locals and tourists.
