Guangzhou

HuaCheng Square

4.0 ★★★★

Huacheng Square is the largest public square in Guangzhou, located in the heart of Zhujiang New Town. It is a modern urban landmark and a popular gathering place for both locals and tourists. Surrounded by skyscrapers, cultural buildings, and shopping centers, the square offers a perfect combination of city life, leisure, and culture. Huacheng Square is known for its wide open spaces, beautiful landscaping, and fountains. It is often used for festivals, events, and public performances, making it a vibrant cultural and social hub in the city.

Top Attraction

Huacheng Square has many attractions that make it a must-visit destination in Guangzhou. The open space of the square is perfect for walking, taking photos, or simply enjoying the city skyline. Its fountains and landscaped gardens create a relaxing atmosphere, especially in the evenings when the lights illuminate the area. Nearby, visitors can explore several notable landmarks, including Canton Tower, which provides panoramic city views, and Guangzhou Library, a modern architectural marvel. The square is also close to Guangzhou Opera House and Zhujiang New Town skyscrapers, forming a cultural and architectural district that combines art, leisure, and city life.

History

Huacheng Square was developed in the early 2000s as part of Guangzhou’s effort to modernize Zhujiang New Town. The square was designed to be a central public space that would integrate culture, business, and tourism. Over the years, it has become a symbol of Guangzhou’s rapid urban development and modernization. Today, Huacheng Square hosts major public events, concerts, and festivals, attracting both residents and international visitors. Its combination of open space, cultural landmarks, and city views has made it one of the most recognizable and visited locations in Guangzhou.
