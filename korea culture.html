Korea

✖

Always accept food or drinks offered by elders.

✖

Avoid showing the soles of your feet when sitting.

✖

Don't speak casually to elders; always use polite language.

✖

Never stick chopsticks upright in rice; it resembles a funeral ritual.

✖

Always take off your shoes when entering someone's home.

✖

Respect local traditions in temples

✖

Don't touch someone's head, especially children's.
