Hotel Villa Fontaine Grand Haneda Airport

5.0 ★★★★★

Property Description

Stay right inside Tokyo's busiest gateway — Haneda Airport — at Hotel Villa Fontaine Grand Haneda Airport, where convenience meets comfort. With direct access to Terminal 3 and seamless connections to trains, buses, and Tokyo's major travel hubs, you'll be moments away from everywhere you want to go — perfect for arrivals, departures, or a transit night.

Step into thoughtfully designed rooms: from cozy doubles to spacious family or tatami‑style Japanese‑Western hybrids, each room offers quality bedding, air conditioning, a flat‑screen TV, mini‑fridge, electric kettle, high‑speed Wi-Fi, and modern private bathrooms everything you need to recharge after flights or sightseeing.

The hotel pampers you with more than just sleep: unwind in a rooftop hot‑spring spa with indoor/outdoor baths, saunas and stone‑bath options — some offering views of airplanes and even Mount Fuji on a clear day.

Onsite dining and amenities elevate the stay: enjoy buffet breakfast, à‑la‑carte meals, a lounge bar, and multiple restaurants for Japanese or international cuisine. For convenience, there's a business center, luggage storage, laundry/dry‑cleaning services, 24‑hour front desk, secure parking, and easy access to shopping and transport.

Whether you're arriving late at night, catching an early flight, or exploring Tokyo, Hotel Villa Fontaine Grand Haneda Airport combines convenience, comfort, and a touch of Japanese hospitality — the ideal base for a seamless start or end to your journey.

History

Hotel Villa Fontaine Grand Haneda Airport opened on December 21, 2022 as part of the Haneda Airport Garden development directly connected to Haneda Airport's Terminal 3. It is one of Japan's largest airport hotels with over 1,500 rooms, designed to serve international and domestic travelers with convenient access to transport, event spaces, dining, and wellness facilities. The hotel was later added to the WorldHotels Distinctive Collection in 2023, strengthening its presence as a high‑quality stay option near one of Tokyo's busiest international gateways.
