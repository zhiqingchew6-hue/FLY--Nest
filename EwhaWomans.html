Ewha Womans University

4.0 ★★★★
Introduction ▶
Introduction Top Attractions History Hotels Traditional Food

Ewha Womans University is a prestigious private university located in Seoul, South Korea. It is one of the oldest and most famous women’s universities in the world, known for its beautiful campus and academic excellence. The university attracts students, visitors, and tourists who are interested in its history, architecture, and cultural significance.

Top Attraction

Ewha Womans University is renowned for its modern and historic campus buildings, scenic walkways, and gardens. The Ewha Campus Complex (ECC) is a notable architectural landmark featuring underground facilities integrated into the hillside. Visitors can explore the campus, take photos of the iconic main gates, and enjoy nearby streets filled with cafés, shops, and boutiques popular among students and tourists. The combination of education, culture, and beautiful surroundings makes Ewha a unique destination in Seoul.

History

Ewha Womans University was founded in 1886 by American missionary Mary F. Scranton as the first educational institution for women in Korea. It started as a small school and gradually developed into a full university offering diverse programs and degrees. Today, Ewha remains a symbol of women’s education, empowerment, and cultural heritage in Korea, while its picturesque campus continues to attract visitors from around the world.
